About JH TOTAL CHILD ACADEMY

Inspiring minds. building Futures.

Founded in April 2009 by MR. and Mrs. Abioye in Dafara, Kuje, Abuja the school began with 11 children and two dedicated teachers. over the years, we have grown into a thriving academic community to excellence in education and character-building.

Our Mission

"Our mission is to provide a nurturing and holistic educational environment that fosters the development of children's moral, physical, intellectual, social, and emotional well-being. We aim to instill a strong sense of responsibility, critical thinking, and compassion, empowering them to become confident, capable, and caring individuals who make a positive impact in the world."

Our Vision

Our vision is to groom children to be sound morally, physically, intellectually, socially and emotionally. To sow a seed of high level of responsibility as they grow to become adult.

Our Founders

Mr. and Mrs. Abioye are passionate educators who envisioned a learning enviroment where children are empowered, creative, and morally upright. their leadership and commitment have shaped the school's journey since 2009.
